Privacy Policy for WatermarkMe - Image Enhancer

This Policy applies to all users of WatermarkMe - Image Enhancer, including those who visit our website or use our app. We understand the importance of protecting user privacy and are committed to ensuring that the information collected is handled securely and in compliance with applicable data protection laws. By using our app, you consent to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

We collect and store only the necessary information to provide you with the App's functionalities. When you install and use the App, we may collect the following information:

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

Data Retention:

We retain your product data and store information for as long as necessary to provide you with the App's services.

Data Security:

We prioritize the security of your information and take appropriate measures to protect it. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Third-Party Access:

We take your privacy seriously, and we do not share your personal information or product data with any third parties except as described in this Privacy Policy. Your data is treated with utmost confidentiality and is not sold, traded, or leased to any external parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Changes to this Privacy Policy:
