Bloom

Welcome to the Bloom Shopify theme documentation. Bloom is a fully-featured theme that has been designed to provide you with a flexible, customizable, and responsive platform for your online store. In this document, we will cover the features of the Bloom theme and explain how you can make the most of its capabilities to create an exceptional shopping experience for your customers. It is fully compatible with the featured of Online Store 2.0.

Summary

Bloom is a powerful and versatile Shopify theme that is perfect for any type of online store. It features a dynamic layout, customizable headers for desktop and mobile, responsive design, powerful product filters, media grid, FAQ, and 15+ custom sections that you can use to create a unique and engaging shopping experience for your customers. Bloom also supports video sections, quick add to cart, secure payment options, smooth animations, and optimized performance, making it an ideal choice for businesses of any size.

Features

Dynamic Layout

Bloom's dynamic layout allows you to easily customize the appearance of your store. With a few clicks, you can choose from a range of preset layouts or create your own unique design. The theme includes a range of customizable sections, such as:

Sections

Featured product

With the Featured Product section, merchants can create eye-catching displays that effectively promote their products and increase sales.

Slideshow

With the Slideshow section, merchants can create visually appealing and interactive displays that leave a lasting impression on their customers. This section is commonly used on the homepage, landing pages, or promotional sections to showcase multiple images or messages in a dynamic and eye-catching manner.

Featured collection

Featured Collection section is a customizable section that allows merchants to showcase a specific collection of products prominently on their online store. It provides an opportunity to highlight selected products, promotions, or special collections to attract customer attention and drive sales.

Collection list

Collection List section is a versatile component that allows merchants to display multiple collections of products on their online store. It enables them to showcase a range of curated collections or product groups in a visually appealing and organized manner.

Image with text

Image with Text section allows merchants to display a combination of image and accompanying text on their online store. It provides an engaging way to present information, tell a story, or highlight specific message.

Icon with text

Image with Icon section allows merchants to display store information. Example can be shipping & returns, support etc.

Product list

Product List section is a section that displays a list of products.

Multicolumn

A section with multiple columns. It provides a way to organize and display various content, such as product collections, blogs products all together in a single place.

Multirow

A section with multiple rows. It provides a way to organize and display various content, such as product collections, blogs products all together in a single place.

Testimonial

Testimonial section is a powerful feature that allows merchants to showcase customer testimonials and reviews or any other information on their online store. It provides social proof and builds trust by featuring positive feedback from satisfied customers.

Video

Video section allows merchants to incorporate videos into their online store. It provides an engaging way to showcase product demonstrations, brand stories, promotional videos, or any other video content to captivate and inform customers.

Featured blog

Featured Blog is another effective effective section that allows merchants to showcase and highlight specific blog posts on their online store. It provides a platform to draw attention to valuable content, news, updates, or articles that can engage and inform customers.

Contact form

Include contact form in any page. This section provides a convenient way for customers to reach out, ask questions, provide feedback, or inquire about products and services.

Countdown

Countdown section is a customizable component you can add to your Shopify store to create a sense of urgency and encourage customers to take immediate action, such as making a purchase or signing up for a promotion. It typically displays a countdown timer that indicates the time left until a specific event, sale, or offer ends.

Richtext

With the Rich Text section, you can easily add and style content to communicate your brand's message effectively.

Collapsible content

This section allows you to create a content block that users can expand or collapse to reveal or hide additional information. This feature is particularly useful when you want to provide detailed information or instructions without overwhelming the user with a long block of text.

Image banner

Allows you to prominently display an image with a text overlay or a call-to-action button on your store's pages. This section is often used to create visually appealing and attention-grabbing banners for promotions, announcements, or showcasing products.

Collage

A collage section is a visually appealing component that allows you to display image, video, collection, product all together in a creative and organized layout.

Stack image

Display a series of images, creating a visually appealing and organized presentation. This type of section is often used to showcase a collection of products, highlight features, or create an eye-catching visual element on your store's pages.

Autoplay text

An autoplay text section is a dynamic element that displays a series of text content items one after the other automatically.

Page

A page section is to showcase your different page content.

Newsletter

Another bloom theme feature that allows merchants to collect email addresses and build a subscriber base for their newsletter or email marketing campaigns. It provides an effective way to capture leads, engage customers, and promote products and updates through regular newsletters.

Responsive Design

Bloom is fully responsive, which means it looks great on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. The theme automatically adjusts the layout and design to ensure your store looks its best on any device.

Quick Add to Cart

Has 2 cart type: Page, Drawer

Multiple Language Support

Bloom could be enhanced with multi-language support to enable merchants to sell products in multiple languages. This feature would allow merchants to translate their store content into multiple languages and provide a seamless shopping experience for customers who speak different languages. Supported languages are English, Bengali, French, Japanese, Spanish, Arabic and German.

Bloom includes a customizable FAQ page that allows you to provide answers to commonly asked questions. You can customize the appearance of the FAQ section to match the design of your store.

It helps set clear expectations and builds trust by providing transparent information on how products are shipped and how customers can initiate returns or exchanges.
